## Architectural Rationale: Why the Unified Cashbook?
Rather than creating separate tables for Bank accounts, cash boxes, daily ledgers, and voucher registries, the system uses a **Single Ledger Architecture** represented by the `cashbook` key-value store:
1. **Double Entry Integration:** Every single payment (Debit) and receipt (Credit) is logged in the cashbook. The system dynamically classifies cash vs bank accounts using the `payMode` attribute of each record.
2. **Dynamic Report Calculations:** Because all transactions exist in a single array, calculating Daily Registers, Bank Statements, Cash Books, and Balance Sheets does not require complex JOIN queries across multiple SQL tables. Instead, reports aggregate and compile snapshots in real-time in the client's browser, eliminating database lag.
